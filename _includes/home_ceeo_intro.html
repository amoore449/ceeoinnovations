Welcome to the CEEO

The Tufts Center for Engineering Education (CEEO) is dedicated to improving education through engineering (and vice versa). Our work spans a variety of disciplines from collaborating with Lego Robotics to designing Makerspaces to bringing technology into K-12 classrooms. CEEO Innovations is a database for the many projects and innovations going on at the center.
